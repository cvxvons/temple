FAQ

Common Questions

Is the app free to use? +
The app is free to download. Some podcast episodes and premium content require a one-time purchase, but a large library of content is freely available to all users.
Can international users access the app? +
Yes. The app is available globally on both the App Store and Google Play. Content is currently available in Mongolian, with English content expanding.
Is it available on Android and iOS? +
Yes — Philosophy.mn is available on both Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store). Download it from either platform using the link on this page.
Where do the quotes and content come from? +
All quotes, episodes, and content are carefully researched and sourced from the original works of each philosopher, verified against academic references and primary texts.
